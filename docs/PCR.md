## PCR

**General PCR protocol.** T<sub>a</sub> is usually 48–52ºC.

1. 95ºC, 180s
2. 95ºC, 30s
3. T<sub>a</sub>, 30s
4. 72ºC, 60s
5. Go to step 2 (34 times)
6. 72ºC 300s
7. 12ºC, forever
